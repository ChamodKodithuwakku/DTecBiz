Call for Abstracts

DTecBiz 2026 – SLIIT Kandy UNI

Submission Guidelines

DTecBiz 2026 invites the submission of abstracts on the symposium themes. To ensure uniformity, every author should use the official DTecBiz 2026 abstract template when preparing their abstract.

  • All submitted abstracts are reviewed under a blind review process. The author's name(s), affiliation(s), and corresponding author's email address are disregarded when the abstract is evaluated.
  • Accepted abstracts must be presented on-site during the technical sessions and will be published in the symposium proceedings.
  • Abstracts must be written and submitted in English.
  • The abstract should be submitted in electronic form (both *.docx and *.pdf) to the editorial board via email.
  • The submitting abstract document should include a title, author(s) information and affiliation(s), corresponding author's email address, the Abstract, and Keywords.

Abstract Formatting Guidelines

The abstract should be a summary of the research (maximum one B5 page) including the purpose of the study, the research methods/methodology used, major findings, conclusions, and any policy implications. The document should be prepared in MS Word using the following format:

  • Font: Bell MT, with single (1.0) line spacing.
  • Font size: 14 pts for the title; 12 pts for author name(s), affiliation(s), corresponding email, main text, and Keywords.
  • The main text should be justified on both sides. Please do not use footnotes.
  • Length: should not exceed one B5 page — approximately 350–400 words for the whole document.
  • Use only well-known acronyms/abbreviations; scientific names should be italicised.
