Welcome to DTecBiz 2026

The Symposium on Emerging Digital Technologies and Business Systems (DTecBiz – 2026) is hosted by SLIIT Kandy UNI as a platform for undergraduate research dissemination, networking, and academic development. Guided by the theme “From Knowledge Creation to Societal Impact: Pathways for Innovation, Growth, and Well-being”, the symposium brings together students and staff to share emerging research at the intersection of digital technology and business.

DTecBiz 2026 is a physical symposium with on-site presentations and interactions, welcoming Year 2 Semester 2 and Year 3 Semester 1 undergraduates as well as students from both state and non-state universities across the country. Accepted abstracts will be published in the symposium proceedings.

  • To foster and promote a research culture among SLIIT Kandy UNI students and staff.
  • To create a platform for undergraduate research dissemination, networking, and academic development.

Abstract Publishing

DTecBiz 2026 invites the submission of abstracts only. All accepted abstracts will be published in the symposium proceedings and presented on-site during the technical sessions.

Abstracts are evaluated under a blind review process, so author names, affiliations, and corresponding email addresses are disregarded during review. Please note the following:

  • Abstracts must be prepared using the official DTecBiz 2026 abstract template.
  • Each abstract should be a maximum of one B5 page (approximately 350–400 words).
  • Accepted abstracts are published in the symposium proceedings.
