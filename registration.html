Registration Fee

Registration CategoryFee
Student Registration (per abstract)LKR 3,000

The registration fee is charged per accepted abstract. Presenting authors must complete registration to be included in the technical programme and the symposium proceedings.

Eligibility is limited to students currently enrolled in undergraduate studies. Applicants must provide a letter confirming their current student status from their internal supervisor where required.

Payment

Payment details and instructions will be shared with authors upon acceptance of their abstract. For any queries regarding registration or payment, please contact the organizing committee:
